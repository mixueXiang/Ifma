II. Paper Submission Process

Please send Abstracts with no more than 300 words in Chinese (200 words in English) before September 30th, 2015. Please send Papers with no less than 3000 words in English before Octorber 30th, 2015.

Submissions Requirements

Submissions can be written in either Chinese or English. All submissions must be original, unpublished, and not submitted concurrently for publication elsewhere. The final version of the paper must be prepared in , adhering to the WORD format.

Please submit your paper (in Word) titled with "First author name+paper title".

Please send Email titled with "IFMA2015 papers+First author name+paper title" to ifma2015@126.com.

III. Paper Review

All submissions will be reviewed by the academic board of IFMA2015.

Authors will be informed by Email no matter the paper is accepted or not.

Some promising authors will be invited to make presentations at the conference. Please check Emails after October 15th.

All accepted papers will be included in the conference proceedings published by Science Press.

Fvie Excellent Paper Awards will be announced to recognize excellence of the papers in the conference.

And these five papers will be financially supported for their open access fees once they are published in international high-quality journals (SCI / SSCI cited).
