  • Report 1: Negotiation engineering

    Michael Ambuehl

    He is a Professor of Negotiation and Conflict Management at the Department of Management, Technology, and Economics of ETH Zurich. He studied applied mathematics and management science at ETH Zurich and graduated as Dipl. Ing. and Dr. sc. techn. ETH. Following his work as academic assistant, senior assistant and lecturer in the Faculty of Economics of Zurich University and dissertation at ETH Zurich, Michael Ambühl became a member of the diplomatic service of the Federal Department of Foreign Affairs in 1982. He was promoted ambassador in 1999, then State Secretary for Foreign Affairs in 2005, and State Secretary in the Federal Department of Finance (DFF) in 2010. Michael Ambühl was nominated full Professor of Negotiation and Conflict Management at ETH Zurich in summer 2013. He negotiated the Swiss-US UBS-deal, the Tax treaty Switzerland-UK&Austria, the Swiss-US FATCA Agreement and the Swiss-US Tax deal.
